This module shows the history of previously confrimed orders in popover, while clicking on the button which is located in sale order line. And also provides pivot view for a particular product.

While clicking on the clock button, it opens a popover. The popover contains the information of last 10 confirmed orders with the details of order's customer,order confirmed date product's unit price and quantity of selected product. It also contains one button at the bottom. which redirects to the pivot view of sales orders.

Pivot view has some customization. When user redirects to the pivot view, it has been group by the customer of a product, group by of confirmed date in interval of year and month.
